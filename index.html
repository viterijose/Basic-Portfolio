About me

My name is Jose Viteri and ever since I was a kid I dreamed of becoming an inventor.
What started as a child's dream has now become a career, with the knowledge I will
acquire from this course I wish to come closer to inventing, not just electrical or
mechanical gadgets, but also websites and apps that may let me control them.

Besides me dreaming of becoming an inventor, I really enjoy going to the gym,
trying out new restaurants and enjoying a nice glass of beer while watching my
two pups play in my backyard.
